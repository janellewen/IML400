Wireframes

My site will be more graphic-based and navigation will be consistent throughout the pages. It will be represented in 4 squares that make a square, each highlighted when the user is on the section of the website. The use of the geometric shapes breaks up the space and helps the user digest the information easily. In addition, there is more hierarchy cascading from top down.
